Our Story

H2flo is a group of 6th graders passionate about the UNSDG goal #10 (clean water and sanitation). Our school gave us the amazing opportunity of doing a school project on it.

Our kit features a water filter to get rid of the dirt and rocks, and then an eco tab to kill the bacteria.
